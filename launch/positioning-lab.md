## 🎯 Research Inputs (informing all variants)

**Words to KILL in 2026:** validate, insights, AI-powered, "brutal" alone
**Words that WIN:** verdict, truth, pivot, "tells you no", time promise, "before you waste months"
**Winning PH pattern:** mechanic + time + emotional stakes (2 of 3 minimum)
**Proof point:** "The cofounder who actually tells you no" → 1,200 PH upvotes (Lovable Critic, 2025)
**Whitespace:** "AI investor verdict" framing — no direct competitor uses it
**Hero output visibility:** Winners SHOW a sample verdict on landing; losers hide it

## 🅰️ VARIANT A — "The Character"

**Tone:** Opinionated, branded, viral-shaped
**Pattern proof:** Lovable Critic (1,200 PH upvotes)

### English
- **Pain hook:** Every tool says your idea is great. This one won't.
- **H1:** The AI investor who tells you no.
- **H2:** Get a GO, PIVOT, or DON'T verdict on your startup idea in 30 seconds — with the 3 reasons why.
- **CTA:** Get my verdict →

### Turkish
- **Pain:** Her araç "harika fikir" diyor. Bu demiyor.
- **H1:** Sana "hayır" diyen AI yatırımcı.
- **H2:** Fikrine 30 saniyede GO, PIVOT veya DON'T kararı — ve 3 sebebi.
- **CTA:** Kararımı al →

### Trade-offs
- ✅ Strongest brand / most viral-shaped
- ✅ Character travels on social (quote-tweetable)
- ⚠️ Character can overshadow mechanic
- ⚠️ Some founders may read it as too edgy for SaaS audience

**Pick this if:** You want Council to become a *brand*, not just a tool.

**Choice:** [ ] Yes  [ ] No

## 🅱️ VARIANT B — "The Founder's Question"

**Tone:** Empathetic, pain-first, outcome-led
**Pattern proof:** FounderPal Validator (1,100 PH upvotes), classic outcome-led pattern

### English
- **Pain hook:** Before you waste 6 months building — ask.
- **H1:** Should you build it?
- **H2:** Paste your idea. Get a GO, PIVOT, or DON'T verdict in 30 seconds. Backed by 3 evidence-based reasons.
- **CTA:** Get the verdict →

### Turkish
- **Pain:** 6 ay inşa etmeden önce — sor.
- **H1:** Bunu inşa etmeli misin?
- **H2:** Fikrini yaz. 30 saniyede GO, PIVOT veya DON'T. 3 kanıta dayalı sebeple.
- **CTA:** Kararı al →

### Trade-offs
- ✅ Highest "I get it" rate — mirrors founder's inner monologue
- ✅ Low-risk, safe to launch
- ⚠️ Less memorable / less quote-tweetable
- ⚠️ No character = no brand moat

**Pick this if:** PH launch clarity is the #1 priority and you want zero positioning risk.

**Choice:** [ ] Yes  [ ] No

- **H1:** GO. PIVOT. DON'T.
- **H2:** The brutal startup idea verdict you need before you write a single line of code. 30 seconds, 3 reasons, zero sugarcoating.
- **CTA:** Get my verdict →

### Turkish
- **Pain:** Arkadaşların söylemeyecek. VC'ler toplantıya almayacak. Biz söyleriz.
- **H1:** GO. PIVOT. VAZGEÇ.
- **H2:** Tek satır kod yazmadan önce ihtiyacın olan acımasız fikir kararı. 30 saniye, 3 sebep, sıfır yumuşatma.

### Trade-offs
- ✅ Maximum differentiation — owns the wedge
- ✅ H1 is instantly scannable / memorable

- ⚠️ Slightly higher "huh?" risk if pain hook is missed

**Pick this if:** You believe GO/PIVOT/DON'T is the product's soul and want to lead with it.

6. **[OPTIONAL] "Verdict + next step"** — competitors bundle fixes with verdict; retention lever
