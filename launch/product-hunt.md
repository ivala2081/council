# Product Hunt Listing — Council

## Tagline
Your startup idea deserves the truth, not encouragement

## Description
Council is the AI investor who's seen 10,000 pitches. Describe your idea in one sentence — get a GO, PIVOT, or DON'T verdict in 30 seconds.

No 50-page report. No corporate speak. Just 3 evidence-backed reasons with real market data.

**What you get:**
- A verdict: GO / PIVOT / DON'T — with confidence score
- 3 reasons backed by live market research (not hallucinated)
- Financial snapshot: MVP cost, breakeven, pricing
- Tech feasibility: stack, complexity, timeline
- Legal flags if any
- A shareable verdict card with OG image

**What makes it different:**
- Uses real-time web search (Exa) — not training data guesses
- Brutal honesty with evidence, not vibes
- Re-submit to get delta analysis ("what changed since last time")
- Bilingual: English + Turkish

Built by a solo founder. Open source.
